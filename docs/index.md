# Drivest Documentation

Drivest is a vehicle cost and fuel tracking app for iOS. All your data stays on your device — no accounts, no servers, no tracking.

## Table of Contents

- [Getting Started](getting-started.md) — First launch, adding your first vehicle
- [Fuel Tracking](fuel-tracking.md) — Logging fill-ups, receipt scanning, fuel efficiency
- [Cost Tracking](cost-tracking.md) — Recording expenses by category
- [Reminders](reminders.md) — Date-based and distance-based reminders
- [EV Features](ev-features.md) — Electricity bills, EV tracking
- [Statistics](statistics.md) — Spending summaries, odometer charts
- [Currency](currency.md) — Default currency, additional currencies, exchange rates
- [Connected Services](connected-services.md) — Vehicle manufacturer integrations, Shortcuts
- [Backup & Restore](backup-and-restore.md) — Export and import your data
- [Settings](settings.md) — Appearance, language, categories, vehicle order
- [Privacy Policy](privacy.md) — How your data is handled
- [FAQ](faq.md) — Frequently asked questions and troubleshooting

## Requirements

- iOS 17.0 or later
- iPhone
