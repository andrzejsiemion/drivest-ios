# Statistics

The **Statistics** tab provides a visual overview of your vehicle's costs and usage over time.

## Odometer Chart

The chart at the top shows your odometer readings and spending history. You can switch between chart types:

- **Odometer** — how your total distance has changed over time.
- **Costs** — your spending over time.

Use the period selector to filter the chart by time range.

## Spending Summary

Below the chart, a summary section shows key metrics for the selected period:

- **Total Spent** — the total amount spent on fuel in the period.
- **Total Fuel** — the total volume of fuel added.
- **Fill-Ups** — the number of fill-up records.
- **Avg Efficiency** — the average fuel efficiency (e.g. L/100km).

If you have a default currency set, amounts are displayed in that currency. Fill-ups recorded in other currencies are converted using the exchange rate at the time of the fill-up.

## Switching Vehicles

Use the vehicle picker at the top of the Statistics tab to view data for a specific vehicle. Each vehicle has its own independent statistics.

## Time Periods

The chart period selector lets you zoom in on different time ranges to compare spending across weeks, months, or the full history of your vehicle.
